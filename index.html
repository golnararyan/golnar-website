About Me

My name is Golnar Aryan. I’m an architect with a Master’s degree from Politecnico di Milano and over seven years of professional experience in Germany, as well as one year in Italy. I am passionate about designing thoughtful, sustainable, and functional architecture that seamlessly blends aesthetics and technical precision.

My expertise lies in the early to detailed design phases (LPH 1–5), where I’ve worked on a wide range of projects — from residential renovations to new data center developments. I bring a creative mindset, strong organizational skills, and a deep attention to detail to every project I take on.

I follow a holistic planning approach, supported by solid proficiency in BIM methodology (modeling, coordination, and management), and technical tools such as Revit, AutoCAD, Vectorworks, and SketchUp. I value teamwork and client collaboration — because I believe the best architecture comes from shared ideas and clear communication.

Outside of architecture, I recharge and find new inspiration through painting, traveling, and sports — passions that also inform and enrich my design perspective.
